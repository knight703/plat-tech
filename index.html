ABOUT US

It is a town in Pangasinan, Philippines, located by the sea and serving as the provincial capital. People in Lingayen mostly farm, fish, and run small businesses. The town is known for its long white-sand beach and the historic St. William the Hermit Church. Lingayen played an important role in World War II, especially during the Allied landing in 1945. The land is mostly flat, and the weather is tropical with rainy and dry seasons.

Why Visit Lingayen?

Visit Lingayen for its serene beaches, local culture, and delicious seafood. It's a perfect spot for relaxation and sightseeing.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is from November to May when the weather is dry and sunny, ideal for beach activities.
